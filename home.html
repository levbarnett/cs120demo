About Us

We are a band formed in Boston, MA in late 2023. We have 3 members, all of whom love music more than (almost) anything! We play over 15 instruments between us and would love to share our music with you!

What We Play

We mainly play covers of popular songs, but we also love working on some of our own. See a list of our favorite covers on our Songs page, along with the titles of our original songs!

Where We Play

Anywhere you want! We mainly do weddings and Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, but we're open to any kind of event where you're wanting a private band. Send us a message on our Contact page and let us know!
