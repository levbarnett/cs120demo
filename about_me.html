Lev Barnett

Over spring break, I went to Myrtle Beach, SC with the Tufts frisbee team. After months and months of the Medford/Somerville chilly weather,
it was amazing to spend the week on a beach. The whole week was blissfully warm, even when it rained. Although Myrtle Beach isn't the nicest area
and it didn't have a lot going on, it was heavenly to just sit in the sun all day.

My Favorite Pet Names:

My favorite restaurant is Nijiya in Medford because their sushi is ready quickly, but it's still delicious.
They have rolls that that I could eat for days, which is good because the restaurant is all-you-can-eat!
