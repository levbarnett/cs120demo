Boots

Boots is the heart and soul of the band. With a voice that sounds like it's coming straight from an angel, she can lead you to any musical destination. She's been known to play some instruments, but her home is at the microphone, dropping jaws and breaking hearts.

Hooligan 1

Hooligan 1 is our resident musical genius. After picking up the violin at age 3, he quickly moved on to the entire rest of the orchestra. He's been known to play the guitar and paino as well (at the same time!) and he's the secret spice behind our sound.

Hooligan 2

Hooligan 2, aka The Metronome, has an extraordinary ability to keep a steady beat. Rivaled only by such experts as George Michael Bluth, his internal clock keeps our band on the beat day after day. We're not quite sure that he's actually human, but here's to hoping!
